MBS IS HERE TO HELP YOU

Modern Bridal Showcase will offer all the services
and tips you need to plan your dream wedding.
  • Bring a note pad and have your questions written down so you won’t forget.
  • Gather information and compare services
  • Collect any information you are interested in - take it home to look over again when you have some quiet time.
  • Bring a pen and print very clearly when registering for anything.
  • Take notes. This will help you remember your favorite vendors.
  • Bring your checkbook if you decide to book any services, or hold dates with a deposit. Be sure to check on refund policies before giving any money, and read the contract carefully before signing.
  • If you are out of town, send your mother or friend to register you for prizes and collect information on services you need.
  • Don’t forget to attend the fashion show!
  • Make sticker labels with your personal contact information (to include: name, address, phone number, email, wedding date, venue) to save you time and hassle when registering with vendors.

QUESTIONS TO ASK

Our wedding professionals will save you time, money and energy!
So get your list of questions ready in the following areas:
  • FINANCIAL: What are four areas of agreement that a couple needs for a joyful marriage?
  • OFFICIATING: How much time in advance should one allow to obtain a marriage license?
  • CONSULTING: Why get more than just day of coordination services?
  • DECOR: What are the popular color schemes that can match my wedding theme?
  • PHOTOGRAPHY: What can you do to prepare for your wedding day images?
  • BEAUTY: Outdoor photos? Indoor photos? Will you still look the same on both photos?
  • ENTERTAINMENT: How can I balance music between my friends and my parents' friends?
  • FLORALS: What flowers are best to consider during your wedding time in the year?
  • DESSERTS: What is the difference between a fondant cake and a butter cream cake?
